Who We Are

Driving Innovation in Technology

ACM VVIT is a vibrant student chapter dedicated to fostering learning, innovation, and collaboration in the field of computer science and technology. We are part of the world's largest educational and scientific computing society.

Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to enhance their technical skills through workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and guest lectures from industry experts.

Join our community of passionate learners and innovators who are shaping the future of technology.
